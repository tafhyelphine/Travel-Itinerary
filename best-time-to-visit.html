Best Time to Visit Rajasthan

Rajasthan is a year-round destination, but the best time to visit is during the cooler months from October to March.

Seasonal highlights include:

  • Winter (October to March): Ideal for sightseeing and exploring the desert. Pleasant weather with cool mornings and evenings.
  • Summer (April to June): Hot temperatures, but a good time to experience local festivals. Suitable for budget travelers with off-season rates.
  • Monsoon (July to September): Scenic beauty with lush landscapes. Ideal for those who enjoy a quieter, less crowded experience.

Plan your trip according to your preferences for weather and activities.
