Wildlife Safaris and Zoos in Rajasthan

Rajasthan offers a unique opportunity to experience wildlife in its natural habitat. Here are some top spots for wildlife enthusiasts:

1. Ranthambore National Park

Ranthambore is one of the best places in India to see Bengal tigers in the wild. It is also home to a variety of other wildlife species and birds.

2. Sariska Tiger Reserve

Sariska Tiger Reserve is known for its population of Royal Bengal Tigers. It also hosts a variety of other wildlife, including leopards, deer, and birds.

3. Keoladeo National Park

Also known as Bharatpur Bird Sanctuary, Keoladeo National Park is a UNESCO World Heritage site famous for its rich avian population.

4. Jaipur Zoo

Located in Jaipur, the zoo houses a variety of animals, birds, and reptiles. It is a good place for families and children to learn about wildlife.

5. Jhalana Leopard Safari

Situated near Jaipur, Jhalana is a great place to spot leopards in their natural habitat. It also offers sightings of other wildlife like deer and peacocks.

Embark on these safaris and zoo visits to experience the rich biodiversity of Rajasthan.
