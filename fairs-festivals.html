Fairs & Festivals in Rajasthan

Rajasthan is renowned for its vibrant fairs and festivals, celebrating the rich culture and traditions of the region.

Explore the colorful events like:

  • Pushkar Camel Fair
  • Jaipur Literature Festival
  • Desert Festival in Jaisalmer
  • Kite Festival
  • Gangaur Festival

Each festival offers a unique glimpse into Rajasthan's heritage, with music, dance, handicrafts, and more.
