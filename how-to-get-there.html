How to Get to Rajasthan

Rajasthan is well-connected by air, rail, and road. Here are the best ways to reach Rajasthan:

By Air

The major airports in Rajasthan are located in Jaipur, Udaipur, and Jodhpur. These airports are well-connected to major cities in India and international destinations.

By Train

Rajasthan has a robust railway network with major railway stations in Jaipur, Jodhpur, Udaipur, and Ajmer. Trains connect Rajasthan to cities across India.

By Road

Rajasthan has an extensive road network with well-maintained highways. You can reach Rajasthan by bus, car, or taxi from neighboring states and major cities.

Choose the mode of transport that suits your travel plans and budget.
